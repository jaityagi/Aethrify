FAQ

Questions About Aethrify HR

Is Aethrify HR compliant with Indian labour laws?
+

Yes. Aethrify HR is built for Indian compliance — PF, ESI, Professional Tax, LWF, and TDS are computed automatically. We update the system with every budget change and regulatory update so you're always compliant. Form 16, ECR, and all statutory challans are generated in one click.

Can it integrate with our existing biometric devices?
+

Yes — Aethrify HR integrates with all major biometric brands (ZKTeco, eSSL, Suprema, Realtime) via API and USB pull. If you have a proprietary device, our team provides a custom integration within 5–7 working days.

How long does implementation take?
+

Most companies are live within 5–7 business days. We handle data migration (from Excel, Tally, or any existing HRMS), configuration, and provide training for your HR team. Onboarding is fully managed — you don't need any technical expertise.

Can employees access their data on mobile?
+

Absolutely. Aethrify HR includes a fully featured employee self-service (ESS) mobile app for Android and iOS. Employees can view payslips, apply for leave, check attendance, raise requests, and submit expense claims — all from their phone.

Does it support multiple branches or companies?
+

Yes — the Enterprise plan supports multi-entity, multi-branch setups with separate policies, payroll cycles, and reporting per entity. You get a consolidated group view alongside individual company dashboards. Inter-company transfers are handled natively.

How is our data protected?
+

All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Role-based access control ensures employees only see their own data. We are hosted on ISO 27001-certified infrastructure with daily backups and 99.9% uptime SLA. DPDP Act compliant.
