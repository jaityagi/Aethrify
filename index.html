FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Aethrify Innovation and our services.

What is a white-label platform and how does it benefit my business?
+

A white-label platform is a product built by Aethrify but rebranded and sold under your company's identity. You get a fully functional, battle-tested software platform — with your logo, colours, custom domain, and brand — without years of development risk. It lets you launch a sophisticated digital product in weeks instead of years, at a fraction of the cost of building from scratch.

How long does it take to build a custom mobile app?
+

A typical MVP takes 8–12 weeks from discovery to App Store launch. Full-featured apps with complex backends take 16–24 weeks. We work in 2-week sprints with live demos so you always know where things stand. Timeline depends on feature complexity, integrations required, and feedback cycle speed on your end.

What digital marketing services does Aethrify offer?
+

We offer a full spectrum: SEO, Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO), PPC/SEM, Social Media Marketing, Content Strategy & Creation, Email Marketing, Conversion Rate Optimisation (CRO), and Analytics & Reporting. We tailor a custom channel mix based on your goals and audience — no cookie-cutter packages.

Can I upgrade from a subscription plan to white-label later?
+

Absolutely. Many clients start on our Launch or Scale subscription to validate their use case, then upgrade to the White-Label Enterprise plan as they grow. Migration is seamless — your data stays intact, and we handle all rebranding, custom domain setup, and onboarding for your team.

Do you provide post-launch support and maintenance?
+

Yes — all plans include post-launch support. Launch gets email support, Scale gets priority support with an 8-hour SLA, and Enterprise clients get a dedicated account manager with 24/7 emergency response. We also offer proactive monitoring, security patches, and performance optimisation on ongoing retainers.

What industries do you serve?
+

Fintech, e-commerce, healthcare, edtech, real estate, logistics, hospitality, media & entertainment, professional services, and SaaS. Our approach is domain-agnostic — we bring deep technical expertise and adapt to your industry's regulatory, UX, and performance requirements.

Is your platform secure and compliant?
+

Security is non-negotiable at Aethrify. Our platform uses end-to-end encryption, RBAC, regular third-party penetration testing, and is aligned with GDPR, ISO 27001, and India's DPDP Act. Enterprise clients receive custom compliance documentation and audit reports on request.

What makes Aethrify different from other agencies?
+

Three things: ownership, speed, and honesty. We treat every project as if it's our own business. We ship fast without cutting corners. And we tell you the truth — including when something won't work. You'll always speak directly with senior engineers and strategists, never outsourced account managers or junior-heavy bench teams.
