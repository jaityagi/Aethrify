FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Aethrify Innovation.

What is a white-label platform?
+

A white-label platform is built by Aethrify but rebranded under your company's identity. You get fully functional software with your logo, colours, and custom domain — without years of development risk. Launch in weeks instead of years at a fraction of the cost.

How long does a custom mobile app take?
+

A typical MVP takes 8–12 weeks from discovery to App Store launch. Full-featured apps with complex backends take 16–24 weeks. We work in 2-week sprints with live demos so you always know exactly where things stand.

Do you build custom HRMS and ERP systems?
+

Absolutely. We specialise in custom HRMS (payroll, attendance, leave management, appraisals) and ERP (procurement, inventory, finance, CRM) built exactly around your business processes — not generic off-the-shelf solutions. Every module is tailored to how you actually work.

What digital marketing services do you offer?
+

Full spectrum: SEO, Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO), PPC/SEM, Social Media, Content Strategy, Email Marketing, CRO, and Analytics. We tailor a custom channel mix based on your goals and audience — no cookie-cutter packages.

Do you provide post-launch support?
+

Yes — all engagements include post-launch support. We offer proactive monitoring, security patches, and performance optimisation on ongoing retainers. Enterprise clients get a dedicated account manager with 24/7 emergency response.

What industries do you serve?
+

Fintech, e-commerce, healthcare, edtech, real estate, logistics, hospitality, media, professional services, and SaaS. Our approach is domain-agnostic — deep technical expertise adapted to your industry's regulatory and UX requirements.

Is your platform secure and compliant?
+

Security is non-negotiable. End-to-end encryption, RBAC, regular penetration testing, GDPR, ISO 27001, and India's DPDP Act aligned. Enterprise clients receive custom compliance documentation and audit reports on request.

What makes Aethrify different?
+

Three things: ownership, speed, and honesty. We treat every project as our own business. We ship fast without cutting corners. And we tell you the truth — including when something won't work. You speak directly with senior engineers and strategists.
